Setup Push-Notifications using Telegram

Feedless can push articles to your telegram account, once both apps are connected. Telegrams applies throttling, so some messages might get dropped.

  1. Search for "@feedlessBot" and click on it

  2. Click on the bot and click "Start"

  3. Feedless will greet you with a message to link your feedless account

  4. Click on the link in the telegram message and "authorize" the connection from your browser

  5. You are able to receive messages by feedless for feeds/repositories with approved push messaging (Opt-In)

  6. Now opt-in "push notifications" for your feeds/repositories of interest
